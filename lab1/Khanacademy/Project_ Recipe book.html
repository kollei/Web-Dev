Recipe #1

Nauryz Kozhe
  • Time: 1:30 - 2:00 hours
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
water 4 liter
meat (beef or lamb) 2 kg
water 4 liter
salt 2 spoons
wheat 200 mg
rice 200 mg
kefir(or katyk) 300 mg
butter 1 spoon

How to cook

Step 1: Boil the meat in water with salt..

Step 2: Add cooked wheat and rice.

Step 3: Let it boiled for a few minutes.

Step 4: Remove from heat, add kefir (or katyk) and a little butter.

Strep 5: Mix well and serve warm.

Recipe #3

Zhent
  • Time: 30 - 45 min
  • Serves: 450 gr
Ingredients Quantity
crushed fried millet 300 gr
butter 100 gr
sugar (or honey) 100 gr
milk 200 ml

How to cook

Step 1: Mix the crushed millet with melted butter, sugar, raisins, and a little milk.

Step 2: Press the mixture into a mold.

Step 3: Let it cool, and cut into pieces.
